SECTION 1

League Introduction

Founded in Nov. 2019, the NWFL emulates the NFL as closely as possible. Maturity is a key factor; act like an adult or this league isn't for you.

Coaches & Coordinators

  • Must join as a Custom Head Coach.
  • Coordinators cannot be fired/hired until after the first full season.
  • Banned: Camp Counselor and Ability Stacking.

League Activity

Advanced at 10:59 PM Central (Mon-Wed-Fri). Streaming is required for every game with VODs enabled.

SECTION 2

Gameplay Regulations

THE MOST IMPORTANT SECTION. READ CAREFULLY.

The 4th Down Rule

  • 4th & 3 or less behind the 50.
  • 4th & 6 or less past the 50.
  • One "Coaches Decision" per game (4th & 15 or shorter).
  • Up by 10+ points: You must punt/FG.

Defensive Restrictions

  • Man Blitz: Max 50% Cover 0/1 per game.
  • Sub LB: Max one Safety (90 SPD or lower) at Sub LB.
  • Rushing: Minimum 3 defenders rushing every down.

Blowout Rule (31+)

If leading by 27+ in the 4th, bench starters if they meet stat milestones (QB: 250yd/3TD, etc).

SECTION 3

Roster & Trades

Trade Rules

  • Limit: 3 trades per season.
  • Untradeables: Cornerstones with 2275+ points are off-limits.
  • Rookies: 1st-3rd rounders are off-limits through their first season.

Position Changes

WR to HB: 6'1" or shorter, 75+ Carrying. WR to TE: 6'3"+, 220+ lbs.

SECTION 4

Player Development

SS/XF Limits

Max 7 per team: 3 on Offense, 4 on Defense. Individual stat caps apply to prevent downgrades (e.g., QB 40+ INTs = Normal dev).
