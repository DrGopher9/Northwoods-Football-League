SECTION 1

League Introduction

The NWFL is a Madden franchise league founded in 2019, emulating the NFL as closely as possible. Maturity is key; act like an adult or this league isn't for you.

Coaches & Coordinators

  • Must join as a Custom Head Coach.
  • Camp Counselor ability is Banned.
  • Ability stacking is Banned.
SECTION 2

Gameplay Regulations

THIS IS THE MOST IMPORTANT SECTION. READ CAREFULLY.

The 4th Down Rule

Acceptable circumstances for going for it:

  • 4th & 3 or less behind the 50.
  • 4th & 6 or less past the 50.
  • Down by 24+ in the 2nd half.
  • One "Coaches Decision" per game (4th & 15 or shorter).

Defense & Usering

  • Man Blitz Limit: Max 50% Cover 0/Cover 1 per game.
  • Sub LB: Max one Safety (90 SPD or lower) at Sub LB.
  • Rushing: Minimum 3 defenders must rush on every down.
SECTION 3

Trade Guidelines

  • Limit: 3 trades per season total.
  • New Users: Must wait 3 in-game weeks to trade.
  • Approval: All trades require 3 commissioner thumbs-up via NeonSportz.
  • Untradeables: Players with 2275+ points cannot be traded.
